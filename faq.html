Frequently Asked Questions

What is FBLA?

FBLA stands for Future Business Leaders of America. It is the largest student-run business organization in the U.S., helping students develop leadership and business skills.

Who can join Bonita FBLA?

All Bonita High School students are welcome to join, regardless of grade level or prior experience.

What are the benefits of joining?

Members gain leadership experience, compete in regional/national events, network with professionals, and build essential business skills.

How do I join?

Just click the “Join Now” button in the menu and fill out the registration form!
