FBLA Events & Competitions

Explore the various events and competitions that FBLA members can participate in to develop leadership skills, gain real-world business experience, and compete at local, state, and national levels.

Regional Conference

Compete in various business-related events, engage in workshops, and network with other members in the region.

State Leadership Conference

Attend seminars, interact with industry professionals, and compete in more advanced events at the state level.

National Leadership Conference

Compete at the national level with top students from across the country in various business categories.

National Fall Leadership Conference (NFLC)

Receive leadership training, participate in networking opportunities, and attend workshops to kickstart the year.

FBLA Week

Engage in activities that promote FBLA, business, and leadership through community service and school presentations.

Competitive Events

Test your skills in Business Calculations, Public Speaking, Marketing, and other business-related categories.

Workshops & Training

Participate in workshops on entrepreneurship, public speaking, and marketing to build essential business skills.

Leadership Training Events

Develop essential leadership skills through various training events tailored to help you grow as a leader.
